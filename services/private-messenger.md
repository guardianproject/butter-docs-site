# Private Messenger

With the Private Messenger service, powered by Delta Chat, you can exchange secure messages with people you know. [Delta Chat](https://delta.chat/) is a messenger that uses email protocols. It typically requires an internet connection to work. However, if you use Delta Chat through a Butter Box setup, anyone who creates an account through the same Butter Box can send and receive messages with each other.&#x20;



It works similar to a post office. Anytime you join the Box network, new messages can be sent out and you can sync your DeltaChat app to receive new messages.
